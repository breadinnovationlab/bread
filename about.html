About Kathleen

I’m a consultant and career development coach. After more than 15 years of working with for-profit firms, nonprofits, social enterprises, and start-ups, I’ve seen firsthand what works and what doesn’t in the workplace.

Teams and leaders in every sector have seen it all and tried it all, and most are at a loss for how to authentically improve the working experience for everyone.

As a change leader, I know what it takes to build trust, and resolve operational tension that is often in the way.

In addition to changing the workplace, I’m a 20 year avid volunteer. Since 2004, I’ve provided pro bono career development training for at-risk youth and young professionals and I currently volunteer as workshop facilitator for immigrant women with New Women New Yorkers.
