Personal Productivity

1.

Dismantle
Barriers

When it comes to personal productivity, most of us can identify at least a few known barriers. But more often, we never truly take the time to reflect, acknowledge and collaboratively dismantle barriers that hold us back.

The first phase of our work together will create the time, space and partnered reflection that will move us into a place of action.

2.

Get The
Right Tools

The first action we'll take together is to assess and streamline the various platforms you're currently using to run your life. Organizational apps are obviously incredible tools, but they can also leave us feeling scattered if we haven't strategically implemented them into our lives.

Phase two is all about organizing the tools that organize our lives.

3.

Get
Partnered

With barriers dismantled and optimized tech, we'll begin to set our first few routines and commitments that we'll track and remain committed to together. We'll meet once per week and share real time progress in between meetings by using our new tools.

Phase three will create the routines that sustainably support our new commitments.
