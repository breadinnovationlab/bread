Immigrant Support

1.

Get
Resources

One of the best parts of living in the US is the nonprofits and resources that exist to support people who are looking to improve themselves.

Our first step together will focus on helping you connect with and apply for the training and support you need to move your career forward.

2.

Train
Up

Following your first steps of training, we'll partner up to implement and practice what you've learned. Together we'll reflect on what works and what doesn't and make adjustments along the way. By implementing sustainable practices, routines and technology we'll create a new way of managing your career goals.

Phase two will be about practicing, assessing and make improvements to both our approach and ability to commit to your career goals.

3.

Reach
Higher

After your initial career goals have been accomplished, you'll want to reassess and set new goals for the next steps in your professional and personal journey.

Phase three will be about taking stock of what we've accomplished and how we want to cultivate healthy work-life balance that leads to sustainable emotional and physical health.
